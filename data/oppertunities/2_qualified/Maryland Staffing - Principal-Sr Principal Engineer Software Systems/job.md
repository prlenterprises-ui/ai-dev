## Job Description

Job Title: Principal/Sr Principal Engineer Software Systems

Award-winning Northrop Grumman is seeking a Principal/Sr Principal Software Systems Engineer to lead our innovative team in developing cutting-edge technologies for emerging supercomputing applications.

This exciting opportunity will involve:
• Developing and evaluating complex models to simulate system behavior, using languages like Python, C/C++, and SysML/UML
• Analyzing data to identify areas for improvement and optimize system performance
• Executing simulations, managing data, and ensuring accuracy and reliability of the simulation environment
• Designing and optimizing systems using simulation results, with experience in software design patterns and version control
• Collaborating with engineers, scientists, and stakeholders to translate requirements into simulation models and communicate findings
• Creating and maintaining technical documentation, reports, and presentations to support the simulation process and its results

This position requires a strong background in digital engineering, with experience in Model-Based Systems Engineering and Object-Oriented Analysis and Design principles. The ideal candidate will have a bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering or other STEM discipline, with 5-8 years of experience in digital engineering, depending on level. Current active TS/SCI clearance with Poly is also required.
